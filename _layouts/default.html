Key Projects

A Numerical Scheme for the Optimal Liquidation Problem Under Jump Diffusion Dynamics on High-Frequency Data
A research paper that developed an infinite-horizon numerical scheme to model an investor interested in liquidating her position in a pairs trading portfolio under the assumption that the pairs portfolio process exhibits jumps.

JNJ, PFE, and MRNA Corporate Financial Analysis Q1 2021
An analysis of corporate governance, historical risk and return, capital structure, company project characteristics, and dividend policy to provide a valuation and recommendations for each company.

JPM Valuation and Equity Analysis Q3 2021
An analysis of a company's intrinsic value using a dividend discount model, a relative valuation comparing the financial ratios, and a quantitative analysis using time-series forecasting and value-at-risk calculations.

Projects

DJI Optimal Portfolio Allocation Q3 2021
This notebook includes the optimal weights given to stocks for a portfolio with constant capital, constraints such as maximizing returns, and minimizing volatility were added. A Monte-Carlo simulation was used to generate random weighted portfolios to plot an efficient frontier.

Converge Valuation Q2 2021
An intrinsic valuation using the free cash flow to equity model, and a relative valuation through a regression on comparable firms to determine the value of equity in the firm.

Time Series Models
